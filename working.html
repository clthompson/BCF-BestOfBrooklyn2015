Directions

Tribeca Rooftop

165 West 65th Street
New York, NY 10023

By Subway:

Take the #1 local train to the 66th Street station.

By Bus:

The M5, M7, M11, M66 and M104 bus lines all stop within one block of Lincoln Center.

By Car:

Parking is available at the Lincoln Center Park and Lock Garage, which is located on the south side of 65th Street, between Amsterdam and Columbus Avenues. The garage can be reached from Amsterdam Avenue by turning right onto 65th Street.
